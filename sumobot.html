Everyone's Welcome!

The Sumobot team engineers small, autonomous robots designed to track down and push an opposing robot out of a ring. Competing in the annual McMaster Sumobot Competition, the Sumobot Team is a great place to work in a close-knit team to build up your skills in all areas of robotics. The team is open to all students, regardless of experience and major. Join today and discover your new passion for robotics!
